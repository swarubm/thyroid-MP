General Thyroid Health Tips
Meal Timing

Take thyroid medication on an empty stomach, 30-60 minutes before breakfast. Avoid taking it with food, especially high-fiber foods.

Hydration

Stay well-hydrated. Water helps with metabolism and can help with constipation that often accompanies thyroid issues.

Exercise

Regular exercise can help boost metabolism and energy levels. Start with low-impact activities like walking or swimming.

Sleep

Prioritize quality sleep. Thyroid issues can affect sleep patterns, so establish a consistent bedtime routine.

Sample Meal Plan
Breakfast
  • Greek yogurt with berries
  • Oatmeal with nuts and seeds
  • Green tea or herbal tea
  • Avoid coffee for 1 hour after medication
Lunch
  • Grilled salmon or chicken
  • Quinoa or brown rice
  • Steamed vegetables
  • Side salad with olive oil dressing
Dinner
  • Lean protein (fish, chicken, tofu)
  • Sweet potato or quinoa
  • Roasted vegetables
  • Herbal tea before bed
Snacks (Choose 2-3 per day):
  • Nuts and seeds (Brazil nuts, almonds)
  • Greek yogurt with honey
  • Apple with almond butter
  • Carrot sticks with hummus
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Dark chocolate (70%+ cocoa)
